Audex Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Overview

Audex is a desktop application for generating professional financial statements from Excel workbooks. Your privacy is important to us, and Audex is designed with a strict local-only architecture.

Data Collection

Audex does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or user information. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No network requests of any kind
  • No cookies, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting
  • No crash reporting or usage statistics

File Processing

All file processing occurs entirely on your local device. When you import an Excel workbook or generate a PDF, the data never leaves your computer. Audex operates fully offline and does not require an internet connection.

Data Storage

Audex stores application preferences locally on your device using standard operating system mechanisms. No data is stored on external servers or cloud services.

Third-Party Services

Audex does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Audex does not collect any information from any user, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date.
